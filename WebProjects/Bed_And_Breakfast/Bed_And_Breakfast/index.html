Sunken Sea Bed & Breakfast

Your cozy destination to rest beneath the earth.

When it comes to a fine place to spend your time and relax your worries away no place compares to the legendary Sunken Sea! This once thought long lost ocean has rested beneath a harsh desert for untold centuries but with recent excavations it has been discovered and tamed in order to provide a great experience for you our customer.

The Sunken Sea has become home to all kinds of regionally exclusive life from new species of plants and animals to new types of minerals. The local wildlife are all incredibly peaceful though and are happy to interact with anyone that comes nearby.

With that in mind I hope this unique experience and beautiful scenery that you decide to book a stay with us to enjoy everything this secretive resort has to offer.
